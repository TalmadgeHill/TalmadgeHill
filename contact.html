Directions

870 Hollow Tree Ridge Rd
Darien, CT 06820

From the east on the Merritt Parkway:

Take exit 37. At the end of the ramp turn right onto Mansfield Avenue (Route 124 going south). Pass the entrance back onto the Parkway and then take the next right turn onto Talmadge Hill Road. At the first stop sign, the church is on your left.

From the west on the Merritt Parkway:

Take exit 36. At the end of the ramp turn right onto Hoyt Street (Route 106 going south). Take the first left turn and you will see the church on your right when you arrive at the first stop sign.


By MetroNorth Railroad:

From Grand Central take the New Haven Line to the Talmadge Hill Station and walk up the hill (about 400 yards) and the church will be right in front of you.

From Stamford take the New Canaan spur and get off at the Talmadge Hill Station.

Parking at Talmadge Hill Church:

There is plenty of on-street parking available on the east side of Hollow Tree Ridge Road, on the south side of Talmadge Hill Road and along Lapham Road. Also there is space for handicapped and senior parking in the small lot off Talmadge Hill Road with a ramp leading into the rear of the church.

Please make sure your cell phones are silenced as you enter the sanctuary.
